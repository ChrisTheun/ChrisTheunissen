About Me


I am Chris Theunissen, a 24-year-old alumni from Maastricht University.
During my BSc in Economics & Business Economics and my MSc in Business Intelligence & Smart Services, I developed a fascination for data analysis and machine learning. That fascination came to light throughout a forecasting elective during my bachelor programme in which every student was expected to find their own time-series dataset to perform forecasting analysis on. I decided to forecast the US meat production and came to appreciate the hands-on approach analyzing data using EViews. This inspired me to specialize in business analytics during my master programme where I learned to code in R, analyze both time-series and cross-sectional data, build and understand statistical models and work together in interdisciplinary teams on real-life business cases. At the end of my master programme, I wrote my thesis on student performance prediction using long short-term memory networks (LSTMs). This forced me to get more acquainted with the Python programming language as well as deep learning theory. Because the idea of finding hidden patterns in data is so intriguing to me, I am excited to both work on personal data analytics projects as well as find a job in the field of artificial intelligence.
